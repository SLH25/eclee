The Certified Business Communication Professional (CBCP) Program

Empowering Young Entrepreneurs Through Communication Excellence

Introduction

The Certified Business Communication Professional (CBCP) program is an integral part of the Young Entrepreneurs Teaching Initiative (YETI), a transformative project under the EU Erasmus Plus horizontal and priority action. This initiative is designed to enhance employability and entrepreneurial success by developing essential transversal skills. CBCP supports YETI's mission by equipping participants with advanced communication capabilities that are crucial for thriving in today’s business world.

As part of YETI, CBCP focuses on cultivating the communication skills necessary for young entrepreneurs and professionals to articulate ideas, engage stakeholders, and lead effectively. These skills are essential for generating business ideas and launching successful ventures.

Four Levels of CBCP Certification

Foundation Level

"Business Communication Essentials" - Equips participants with fundamental communication skills necessary for effective interaction in professional settings.

Intermediate Level

"Proficient Business Communicator" - Enhances verbal and nonverbal communication abilities, enabling participants to engage effectively in diverse professional contexts.

Advanced Level

"Strategic Communication Specialist" - Focuses on strategic communication techniques, including storytelling and cross-cultural communication, to influence and lead effectively.

Professional Level

"Master of Business Communication" - Develops mastery in advanced communication strategies, addressing complex challenges and leading organizational change.

Alignment with the European Framework

The YETI-CBCP program, aligns with the European Key Competence Framework, particularly the competence of "initiative and entrepreneurship." This involves creativity, innovation, and project management. CBCP strengthens this alignment by enhancing communication skills, enabling individuals to effectively turn ideas into action, manage projects, and seize opportunities

Program Impact and Benefits

  • Entrepreneurship Education:YETI-CBCP program, delivers courses that address all aspects of initiative and entrepreneurship. CBCP ensures that participants can effectively communicate their ideas and initiatives, a key factor in entrepreneurial success.
  • Innovative Methods: The program employs innovative pedagogical approaches, with CBCP focusing on communication proficiency to ensure that learning is engaging and applicable to real-world scenarios.
